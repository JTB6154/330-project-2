Description

The purpose of this project is to combine game art with visualization for their audio, the primary purpose was to create something that looked aesthetically pleasing and was also pleasing in terms of audio. I’m using game art alongside their soundtrack because I like them. The games i’ve pulled from are Pyre, Hollow Knight, and Risk of Rain 2

All art and music belongs to their original owners and were used under fair use. This is a school project and is not meant for general distribution.
